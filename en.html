Privacy Policy of Measures - Primary School

Introduction

Thank you for using the Measures - Primary School application. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy describes our practices regarding the information we collect and use through our application.

No Data Collection

We would like to clarify that Measures - Primary School does not collect any personal data, usage information, or any other form of data from its users. We do not track, store, or share any information about you.

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use or process any personal data. The application is designed to function entirely without requiring data collection.

However, the application does store user-configured information such as the level, number of questions, etc., to facilitate navigation when reopening the app.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The application does not use cookies, web beacons, or other similar technologies to collect information about users.

Information Sharing

The application does not share any information about its users with third parties, whether for commercial purposes or otherwise.

Children

Measures - Primary School is suitable for children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete that information from our systems.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes to this policy will be posted here.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at the following email address: smachsoi@gmail.com.
